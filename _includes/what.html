We’ve spoken with over 30 small business owners about compliance. The problem that resonates with most is they lack confidence they’ve got their employment compliance right. Employment law is forever changing and business owners are often unsure how these changes apply to them. Assuring business owners have got employment compliance right, from the start, mitigates the big potential risks of getting it wrong and gives business owners back confidence so they can focus on running their business.
